# First Operator — Worksheet

> The "first non-you operator" is the single most important person you can identify before 2026-05-24.
> If crypto/momentum clears the CI bar, you need ONE person to validate the
> product works for someone who isn't you. Fill this in before 2026-05-21.

---

## Criteria for the right first operator

A good first operator is someone who:

1. **Already trades or evaluates trades systematically.** Not someone who needs the concept of "edge" explained.
2. **Has their own discretionary judgment.** They won't blindly follow signals — they'll push back, which means you'll learn faster.
3. **Is okay with being a beta.** They understand "this is unproven, your money is at risk."
4. **Will tell you when it's broken.** A friend who'd give a thumbs-up out of politeness is worse than no operator.
5. **Has 30 minutes a week to share what they saw.** No useful insight comes from a "set and forget" tester.

## Deadline

- **2026-05-21** (3 days before the decision): name + commitment from your first operator.
- **2026-05-25** (one day after the decision): if the CI cleared, send them their access. If it didn't, send them the "here's what I learned" thread instead.

## Anti-pattern to avoid

Do **not** announce on social media before you have a named operator. "Looking for beta users" with no commitment in advance produces ten interested-but-flaky people, not one real one. The single named person who said yes in DMs is worth more than a hundred public likes.
